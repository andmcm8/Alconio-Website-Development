Project Overview — Alconio Web Services

Alconio Web Services (Alconio) is a modern digital agency focused on building high-performance websites and creating intelligent AI automation systems for businesses. The brand emphasizes sleek design, advanced technology, and efficiency. The website should communicate professionalism, innovation, and simplicity while guiding visitors toward taking action (booking a call or getting started).

⸻

Business Purpose

Alconio helps businesses:
	•	Build fast, modern, conversion-focused websites
	•	Automate workflows using AI to save time and increase efficiency
	•	Improve their digital presence and operational performance

The overall tone of the site should feel futuristic, clean, and premium, while remaining easy to navigate and action-oriented.

⸻

Brand & Visual Identity

Color Palette
	•	Primary Dark: #02010A (deep black-blue)
	•	Secondary Dark: #04052E (dark navy)
	•	Accent Purple 1: #140152 (deep purple)
	•	Accent Purple 2 (Primary Accent / CTA): #0D00A4 (electric blue-purple)
	•	Contrast: #FFFFFF (white)

Color Usage
	•	Backgrounds: smooth gradients #02010A → #04052E → #140152
	•	Call-to-action buttons & interactive elements: #0D00A4
	•	Primary text: #FFFFFF
	•	Secondary text: rgba(255,255,255,0.7)

Design should feel dark, modern, minimal, and slightly futuristic, with soft glows, smooth transitions, and clean spacing.
