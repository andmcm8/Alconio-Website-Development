First Steps

You've successfully integrated the SDK and set up your account. Congratulations! The first 24 hours are crucial for establishing a baseline for your application's performance. Here's what you should focus on right now.

Your First Hour: Explore the Data

After your first few events flow in, head over to the Real-time Overview. You should see:

  • Active Sessions: Real-time count of users currently on your site.
  • Event Stream: A live feed of actions being performed (page views, clicks, etc.).
  • Geo Distribution: A map showing where your initial traffic is coming from.

Verifying Data Integrity

It's important to ensure the data you're seeing accurately reflects reality. We recommend:

  • Test locally: Trigger events from your development environment and verify they appear in the dashboard.
  • Check Property ID: Ensure you're using the correct property ID in your SDK initialization code.
  • Inspect Network Requests: Use your browser's developer tools to see outgoing requests to `api.alconio.com`.

Inviting your Team

Analytics is a team sport. Don't keep all this data to yourself! Go to Settings > Team to invite your colleagues. You can assign roles like Admin, Editor, or Viewer to maintain control over sensitive data.

A Path to Long-term Success

Over the next few days, keep an eye on these metrics to start seeing trends:

  • Bounce Rate: Are users leaving too quickly?
  • Session Duration: How long are users staying engaged?
  • Performance Score: Is your site consistently fast across different regions?
