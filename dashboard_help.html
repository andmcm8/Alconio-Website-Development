Frequently Asked Questions

General & Getting Started

What is Alconio?

expand_more

Alconio is a next-generation analytics and performance dashboard designed to give you deep insights into your digital infrastructure with a focus on speed and clarity.

How do I get started with the dashboard?

expand_more

Simply log in and follow the onboarding wizard to connect your first data property. Most users are up and running in less than 5 minutes.

Where can I find the quick start guide?

expand_more

You can find it on our Documentation page under the "Getting Started" section.

How do I update my profile information?

expand_more

Click on your avatar in the top right corner and select "Profile" to update your name, email, and preferences.

Can I use Alconio for mobile apps?

expand_more

Yes! We support native iOS and Android SDKs as well as Flutter and React Native integrations.

What browsers are supported?

expand_more

We support the latest versions of Chrome, Safari, Firefox, and Edge. For the best experience, we recommend using a modern Chromium-based browser.

How do I change the dashboard theme?

expand_more

Toggle between Light, Dark, and System modes in the Settings -> Appearance tab.

Is there a mobile app for the dashboard?

expand_more

While we don't have a standalone app, our web dashboard is fully PWA-compatible and can be added to your home screen for an app-like experience.

How do I contact support?

expand_more

Use the "Contact Support" section below for Live Chat or Email options. Pro users also get access to dedicated Slack support channels.

Where is the documentation located?

expand_more

All technical guides and API references are available on our Docs portal.

Account & User Management

How do I invite a team member?

expand_more

Go to settings, select "Team Management", and click "Invite Member". Enter their email and assign a role to send the invitation.

What are the different user roles?

expand_more

We offer Owner, Admin, Editor, and Viewer roles, each with granular permissions based on your organization's needs.

How do I reset my password?

expand_more

Click "Forgot Password" on the login screen, or reset it from your Security settings while logged in.

Can I enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)?

expand_more

Yes, we strongly recommend it. Go to Profile Settings -> Security to enable 2FA via Authenticator App or SMS.

How do I delete my account?

expand_more

Account deletion can be requested from the danger zone in your Account Settings. Note that this action is permanent.

How do I change my account email?

expand_more

For security, email changes require verification of both the old and new addresses. You can initiate this in Profile Settings.

Can I have multiple organizations?

expand_more

Yes, one account can belong to or create multiple organizations. Switch between them using the organization selector in the top bar.

How do I transfer ownership?

expand_more

Ownership transfer can be initiated by the current owner in the "Team Management" settings or via a support request for business enterprise accounts.

Analytics & Data Insights

How is "Active Users" calculated?

expand_more

We count unique users who have initiated at least one session within the selected time window (typically 30 minutes for real-time and 24 hours for daily stats).

What is the difference between "Visitors" and "Users"?

expand_more

"Visitors" are total unique browsers/devices, while "Users" are verified individuals synced across multiple devices via our Identity SDK.

How do I track custom events?

expand_more

Use our `alconio.track('event_name', {metadata})` function in your frontend code. See the Events documentation for more details.

Can I export my analytics data?

expand_more

Yes, all graphs and tables have an export option in the top right. You can choose from CSV, JSON, or PDF formats.

How often is the data updated?

expand_more

Metrics are updated every minute, with historical aggregation occurring every hour for long-term storage.

What is "Bounce Rate"?

expand_more

Bounce Rate is the percentage of single-page sessions in which there was no interaction with the page. A high rate may indicate content relevancy issues or slow load times.

How do I set up conversion tracking?

expand_more

Define "Goal" URLs or events in the Analytics settings. We will then track how many users reach those endpoints vs. total visitors.

Can I see real-time traffic?

expand_more

Yes! The "Overview" page includes a Live Pulse section showing active users on your site right now.

How do I filter data by country?

expand_more

Use the filter bar at the top of the analytics page and select the "Geography" dimension to isolation traffic from specific regions.

What does "User Retention" measure?

expand_more

It measures the percentage of users who return to your site within a specific window (7 days, 30 days) after their initial visit.

Billing & Subscriptions

How do I upgrade my plan?

expand_more

Visit the Billing & Plans page from the profile dropdown to view available plans and upgrade with one click.

What payment methods are accepted?

expand_more

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), PayPal, and Apple Pay through our secure Stripe integration.

How do I download my invoices?

expand_more

Invoices are available for download in PDF format under Billing History on the Billing page.

Is there a free trial available?

expand_more

Absolutely. New users get 14 days of full Pro features for free, no credit card required.

How does "Pay-as-you-go" work?

expand_more

For data usage exceeding your base plan, we bill at a flat rate per 1,000 requests. You can set hard usage limits in settings.

Can I cancel at any time?

expand_more

Yes, subscriptions can be cancelled at any time. You will retain access to your plan until the end of your current billing cycle.

What if I exceed limits?

expand_more

We will never cut off your data. Instead, we transition you to pay-as-you-go rates for the remainder of the month and notify you via email.

Do you offer non-profit discounts?

expand_more

Yes, we offer up to 50% discount for verified educational and non-profit institutions. Contact sales to apply.

Technical & API

Where can I find my API keys?

expand_more

Generate and manage your keys in Settings -> API & Access. Keep these keys secure and never expose them in client-side code.

How do I rotate my API keys?

expand_more

Click the "Rotate" icon next to any active key. We allow a 24-hour overlap where both keys will work to ensure zero downtime.

What is the API rate limit?

expand_more

Default limits are 1,000 requests per minute for Pro users and 5,000 for Enterprise. Limits can be adjusted upon request.

How do I set up webhooks?

expand_more

Register your endpoint URL in Settings -> Webhooks. We support signed payloads for maximum security verification.

Do you support GraphQL?

expand_more

Yes, our Graph endpoint is currently in Beta. You can find schema details and playground links in the Developer portal.

How do I integrate the GA4 proxy?

expand_more

Install our Node.js helper library and set your GA_PROPERTY_ID environment variable. Our proxy handles the OAuth flow automatically.

What is the "System Status"?

expand_more

We monitor global API latency and availability 24/7. Check the Resources page or status.alconio.com for historical uptime data.

How do I report a technical bug?

expand_more

Use the "New Request" button on the Resources page to submit a technical ticket directly to our engineering team.

Security & Compliance

Is my data encrypted?

expand_more

Yes, all data is encrypted both in transit using TLS 1.3 and at rest with AES-256 military-grade encryption.

Is Alconio GDPR compliant?

expand_more

Fully. We offer data residency options in the EU and provide tools for users to exercise their right to be forgotten.

How do I request a DPA?

expand_more

Our Data Processing Agreement is available for digital signature in the Legal section of your organization settings.

Where are servers located?

expand_more

We utilize AWS and Vercel edge networks with primary processing clusters in US-East, EU-Central, and AP-Southeast regions.

Security audits?

expand_more

We undergo SOC2 Type II audits annually and perform quarterly penetration tests with certified third-party security firms.

Report a vulnerability?

expand_more

We operate a private bug bounty program. Please send vulnerability reports to security@alconio.com for coordinated disclosure.
