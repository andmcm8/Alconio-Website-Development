Dashboard Overview

The Alconio Dashboard is your command center for everything related to your application's health and user engagement. It is divided into several key sections to help you find information quickly.

Main Navigation

The sidebar on the left provides quick access to the primary modules of the platform:

  • Overview: A high-level view of your project's current status and key metrics.
  • Analytics: Deep dive into user behavior, session trends, and geographical data.
  • Performance: Monitor core web vitals, response times, and system health.
  • Resources: Access documentation, support guides, and community resources.
  • Settings: Manage your account, team members, and developer preferences.

Header Actions

The top header remains consistent throughout the dashboard, providing context and quick actions:

  • App Selector: Switch between different projects or properties you manage.
  • Notifications: View real-time alerts for performance drops or security events.
  • Profile Menu: Update your personal details or manage your individual account settings.

Sidebar Control

You can expand or collapse the sidebar for more screen real estate. Your preference is automatically saved to your browser, so the dashboard looks exactly how you want it every time you return. Use the arrow icon at the top of the sidebar to toggle between compact and full view.

Primary Content Area

The central portion of the screen is where your data comes to life. Most pages feature "Glass Panels"—semi-transparent containers that elegantly organize charts, tables, and configuration options. All panels are fully responsive and optimized for both desktop and mobile viewing.
