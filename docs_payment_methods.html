Payment Methods

Alconio supports a variety of payment options to ensure users worldwide can easily maintain their subscriptions. We prioritize security and flexibility in all our financial transactions.

Supported Methods

  • Credit & Debit Cards: Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Discover.
  • Digital Wallets: Apple Pay and Google Pay.
  • Bank Transfers: Automated Clearing House (ACH) and wire transfers for Enterprise customers on annual billing.

Adding & Updating Cards

To add a new card, go to Settings > Billing > Payment Methods and click "Add Card". You'll be prompted to enter your card number, expiry date, and CVC. Our system validates the card in real-time. If you're updating an existing card (e.g., because the old one expired), simply add the new card first and then designate it as your primary method.

Primary Payment Method

You can store multiple payment methods in your account. The card marked as "Primary" will be the one used for your monthly or annual subscription renewal. If a payment fails on your primary card, Alconio will automatically attempt to charge your backup methods in the order they were added.

Security & PCI Compliance

Your sensitive payment data never touches Alconio's servers. We use Stripe as our payment processor, a global leader in online payments. When you enter a card number, it is sent directly to Stripe via an encrypted connection, and we only store a secure "token" that allows us to process future payments on your behalf. We are fully PCI-DSS Level 1 compliant.
